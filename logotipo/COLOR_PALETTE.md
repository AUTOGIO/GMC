# GMC Brand Color Palette

**Giovannini Mare Capital LLC**  
**Official Brand Colors**

---

## Overview

This document defines the official color palette for Giovannini Mare Capital LLC, derived from physical brand assets including the Lamborghini Revuelto design language and brand signage.

All colors in this palette serve specific semantic and visual purposes in the brand identity system.

---

## Color Definitions

### 1. Primary: Grigio Acheso (Matte Grey)

**Hex:** `#4A4E52`  
**RGB:** `74, 78, 82`  
**HSL:** `210°, 4%, 31%`

**Visible Application:**
- The body of the Lamborghini Revuelto
- The textured stone background wall

**Description:**
A stealthy, non-reflective deep grey that serves as the "anchor" color for the brand, communicating discretion and resilience.

**Usage:**
- Primary background color
- Body text on light backgrounds
- Neutral surfaces and panels

---

### 2. Secondary: Verde Scandal (Neon Green)

**Hex:** `#D0FF00`  
**RGB:** `208, 255, 0`  
**HSL:** `74°, 100%, 50%`

**Visible Application:**
- The racing stripes on the car
- The brake calipers
- The lower body accents
- The "cut" layers/edges of the GMC logo on the wall

**Description:**
A high-visibility, fluorescent electric green. It represents the "asymmetric opportunity" and "convexity" mentioned in the mandate—sharp, distinct, and impossible to miss against the grey background.

**Usage:**
- Primary accent color
- Call-to-action elements
- Highlights and emphasis
- Chart colors and data visualization
- Focus states and interactive elements

---

### 3. Tertiary: Gloss Black & Carbon

**Hex:** `#0A0A0A`  
**RGB:** `10, 10, 10`  
**HSL:** `0°, 0%, 4%`

**Visible Application:**
- The car's front splitters
- The wheels
- The air intakes
- The deep shadows of the logo's embossing

**Description:**
Used for grounding elements and technical details, adding contrast to the matte grey.

**Usage:**
- Deep background areas
- High-contrast text on light backgrounds
- Grounding elements
- Technical details and shadows

---

### 4. Accent: Metallic Silver (Chrome/Steel)

**Hex:** `#C0C0C0`  
**RGB:** `192, 192, 192`  
**HSL:** `0°, 0%, 75%`

**Visible Application:**
- The face of the "GMC" letters
- The Anchor/Column icon on the wall

**Description:**
A polished metal finish that reflects the environment, adding a layer of institutional weight and luxury to the signage.

**Usage:**
- Logo text and iconography
- Premium accents
- Metallic effects and reflections
- Secondary text on dark backgrounds

---

## Color Hierarchy

1. **Primary (Grigio Acheso)** — Foundation and anchor
2. **Secondary (Verde Scandal)** — Asymmetric opportunity and convexity
3. **Tertiary (Carbon Black)** — Grounding and technical precision
4. **Accent (Metallic Silver)** — Institutional weight and luxury

## Implementation Guidelines

### In Code

- **TypeScript:** Use `dashboard/src/lib/colors.ts` as the single source of truth
- **CSS Variables:** Defined in `dashboard/src/app/globals.css`
- **Tailwind Config:** Extended in `dashboard/tailwind.config.ts`

### Accessibility

- **Contrast:** Verde Scandal (#D0FF00) on Carbon Black (#0A0A0A) provides WCAG AAA contrast
- **Text:** Use Metallic Silver (#C0C0C0) for secondary text on dark backgrounds
- **Focus States:** Use Verde Scandal with sufficient contrast

### Design Principles

- **Asymmetry:** Verde Scandal should be used sparingly and strategically
- **Discretion:** Grigio Acheso dominates for institutional discretion
- **Contrast:** Carbon Black provides grounding and depth
- **Luxury:** Metallic Silver adds institutional weight
